Aaron Pennington, 4 children arrested after womans death, 1 suspect missing in Gardner, Ma

Gardner, Massachusetts – Authorities are launching an urgent manhunt for 33-year-old Aaron Pennington, a Gardner resident, in connection with a tragic incident that unfolded in Gardner, Massachusetts. The incident left a woman dead in her home, prompting an intensive search for the suspect. Law enforcement officials, including the Gardner Police Department and the Massachusetts State Police, are actively seeking Pennington, who has been described as a white male with blonde hair and blue eyes.

The investigation was initiated around 9 a.m. on Sunday when law enforcement responded to a distress call at a residence on Cherry Street. Upon arrival, they discovered the lifeless body of a woman inside the home. While the identity of the deceased has not been disclosed, authorities confirm that she was tragically killed in the incident. The investigation into the circumstances surrounding her death is still ongoing.

Aaron Pennington is not merely a person of interest but a suspect in the case. According to witnesses, he is believed to be armed and dangerous. Pennington is approximately 6 feet 2 inches tall, weighing around 175 pounds. Law enforcement officials have released information that Pennington may be traveling in a white 2013 BMW 320 with Massachusetts Veterans license plate number 8A30.

District Attorney Joe Early, who issued a statement regarding the incident, emphasized the seriousness of the situation. He urged anyone with information about Aaron Pennington’s whereabouts to come forward immediately. Police have maintained a presence on Cherry Street, indicating the gravity of the ongoing investigation.

As the community grapples with this tragedy, authorities are urging citizens to remain vigilant and report any sightings or information related to Aaron Pennington. The search for Pennington continues as law enforcement officers work tirelessly to bring him into custody and seek justice for the victim.

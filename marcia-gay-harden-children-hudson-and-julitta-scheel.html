Marcia Gay Harden Children: Hudson And Julitta Scheel

Marcia Gay Harden, recipient of accolades including an Academy Award and a Tony Award, in addition to nominations for a Critics’ Choice Movie Award and three Primetime Emmy Awards, other than being one fine actress/performer also has a very beautiful family to go home to in real life.

Seeing her play Skylar Astin’s Todd’s mother in uptight perfection in So Help Me Todd (2022), a legal drama series, we have finally thought of telling you all about her children in real life. Here’s what we know.

Who Are Marcia Gay Harden’s Children?

La Jolla, California-born Marcia Gay Harden has three children — a daughter Eulala Grace Scheel and twins Julitta Dee Scheel and Hudson Harden Scheel. Let us tell you about each of them now.

Marcia has opened up about raising her children as a single mom in her first book, The Seasons of My Mother: A Memoir of Love, Family, and Flowers, is bold, funny, and sensitive.

When her marriage was falling apart, she said, her kids knew what was going on and she also did not waste time sugar-coating things and was just pretty straight up with them that their parents are no longer together. Of course, Marcia also wrote here about her divorce and it being painful.

As for being a single mom, Marcia has said there it is really wonderful to be one. In this interview that happened in 2018, she also said she would not want to be in a dysfunctional relationship or think, about it. Yet, she also opened up that despite liking her single life so much, she would open up to love.

Meet Marcia Gay Harden’s Son, Hudson Harden Scheel

Marcia Gay Harden’s son was born along with his twin Julitta Dee Scheel on 22 April 2022.

Earlier that year, their mother had worn a Badgley Mischka maternity gown and 4 inches high heels to the Oscars, where she was nominated for Best Supporting Actress for Mystic River.

About Hudson, if you also need to know, he is gay and uses the pronouns He/Him. It was his mum who herself confirmed to US Weekly about her son’s sexuality. Around that time, attending the Family Equality Council’s Impact Awards meant so much to her because the non-profit advocates for LGBTQ families.

Then, later, during Kelly Clarkson Show, the Fifty Shades of Grey actress also opened up how since the coming out, which she thinks was the bravest thing ever, her son taught her gender pronouns and more.

Hudson appeared on a drag show back in February 2022 and that turned out to be successful. And before that, we saw him on RuPaul’s show with his mum and twin sister. He talked fondly of his mum and his sister. Of course, before that RuPaul introduced him as a budding Hollywood makeup artist and an aspiring YouTube star. Yet, later, Hudson kind of corrected the host that YouTube is not his calling and he is more headed towards broadway. Not to miss, he also told RuPaul that he actually got into doing makeup inspired by his Drag shows.

Most recently, in June 2022, he made his mum proud by graduating from Interlochen Arts Academy.

Meet Marcia Gay Harden’s Daughter, Julitta Harden Scheel

Now, about the twin sister: Julitta Harden Scheel!

In 1996, Marcia Gay Harden married Thaddaeus Scheel, a prop master/filmmaker, and with him welcomed her three children. But, in February 2012, she filed for divorce from her husband.

It was some good years after Eulala Grace was born (in September 1998) that Julitta and her twin brother came around.

Julitta, from the look of things, is an aspiring actress and also has a huge liking for skating. As per LinkedIn, she already has managed to get famous for her roles in Kidding (2018), Code Black (2015), and Trophy Wife (2013).

Apparently, Julitta appeared in her mother’s hit medical drama Code Black during a plane crash crash episode where she shared the screen with her mother. However, her mother later ascertained that her little girl got the audition all by herself. Marcia also claimed that even here she tried not to be a helicopter mum with her.

Many a time, we have also seen Julitta attending the premiere and likewise red carpet events with her seeming momager.

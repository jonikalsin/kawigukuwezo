Lil Wayne popularly known as Dwayne Michael Carter Jr. is an American rapper, entrepreneur and record executive. As of 2023, Lil Wayne’s net worth is $170 million. He is in the industry since the year 1995 and works with the only genre of hip hop.

He is widely recognized as one of the most influential hip-hop musicians of his age and is often named as one of the best rappers of all time by his peers. He works with the labels Young Money, Republic, Universal, and Cash Money.

Lil Wayne Wiki/Biography

Born on 27 September 1982, Lil Wayne’s age is 40 Years Old as of 2023. He was born and brought up in a middle-class Christian family from New Orleans, Louisiana, United States. He holds an American by nationality and has his belife in Christian religion.

He completed his early education at Lafayette Elementary School, McMain Magnet Secondary School, Eleanor McCain Secondary School and Marion Abramson High School in New Orleans, Louisiana, United States. He later enrolled himself at the University of Phoenix, and the University of Houston in Louisiana, the United States from where he completed his graduation.

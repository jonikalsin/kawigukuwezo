Nicole Kidman Rips Up Jimmy Fallon's Interview Questions - Watch!

Nicole Kidman is done with Jimmy Fallon‘s questions!

The 54-year-old Being the Ricardos star made an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon on Wednesday (December 8).

Click inside to read more…

During her appearance, Nicole talked about her Christmas plans and life at home with Keith Urban and her family, as well as celebrating in Australia.

At one point, there was a hilarious moment where Jimmy asked about Nicole singing back-up for Keith, which she awkwardly said never happened, and she began teasing Jimmy. She also started answering a series of holiday questions, and clearly didn’t care for the silly questions. She grabbed the cards, asked Jimmy some questions – and then ripped them up!

She also discussed working with Aaron Sorkin on Being the Ricardos.

Later on, Nicole and Jimmy took turns sticking their hands inside boxes filled with mystery objects that they have to identify without looking in a hilarious game.

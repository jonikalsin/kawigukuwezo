Who Was Luke Roux From Farmington CT? High School Student Passed Away In An Accident

Let’s take a look at “Who Was Luke Roux From Farmington CT?” Luke Roux, a recent graduate from Farmington, Connecticut, passed away on Saturday as a result of a two-car collision. Today, the internet is in sorrow over his passing.

Continue reading if you’re curious to discover more about the young kid Luke, who is now deceased; we’ll fill you in on all the details.

Who Was Luke Roux From Farmington CT?

Luke Roux was a young man who recently took graduation from Farmington High School. As per the sources, the boy was in his 17 at death.

He has participated in the graduation ceremony of Farmington High School with his family and was scheduled to attend the University of Connecticut in the fall.

The recent graduate was a good hockey and baseball player at the high school. The young man remained friendly and generous in his community according to his school. He is the kind of man whose spirit is of giving back to his community for a greater good and consistently stepping up as a leader.

We don’t know if the young man had any social media account like Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter or not. Moreover, the information about his family is not disclosed yet probably, they want to be away from social media touch.

What Happened To Luke Roux? Accident And Obituary

Luke Roux, 17, died on Saturday night due to a car accident. He came between a two-car crash and was seriously injured.

According to nbcconnecticut.com, around 8:30 p.m., police were called to a two-car collision on Colt Highway in Farmington at the intersection of Fienemann Road.

Another youngster Jacob Coffey, 21-year-old driving the next car was also the victim along with Roux. They both were transported to area hospitals for their injuries. Shortly after the collision, Roux was pronounced dead at John Dempsey Hospital, according to authorities.

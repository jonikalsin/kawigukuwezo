Anthony Smith Obituary, Death – Brooklyn, NY – The community is saddened to learn about the passing of Anthony Smith on Saturday, December 30, 2023. B. Anastasio & Son Inc., a respected funeral home in Brooklyn, has officially announced the departure of this cherished individual.

Anthony Smith, whose impact on the community has undoubtedly been profound, will be remembered in a memorial service scheduled to take place on January 6, 2024. The service will be held at B. Anastasio & Son Inc., located at 533 Lorimer Street, BROOKLYN, NY.

The service will conclude at 5:00 p.m., allowing ample time for those who wish to share memories, stories, and reflections on a life well-lived.

Anthony Smith’s presence in the community touched the lives of many, and his passing leaves behind a void that will be deeply felt. The memorial service aims to provide a space for the community to join in collective remembrance, celebrating the positive impact Anthony had on those around him.

As friends and family come together to mourn the loss, the memorial service becomes a beacon of support, fostering a sense of unity and shared grief.

Where Is The Challenge's Casey Cooper Today? Her Husband, Age, Job

The Challenge: All Stars season 2 premiered on Paramount+ in November 2021, and it saw many stars vying to reclaim the top titles. Among them, the Fresh Meat finalist, Casey Cooper, drafted by Wes Bergmann, seemed super serious about winning the competition this time.

But where is Casey today? And what’s going on in her life? Stay till the end as we unveil her personal information.

Where Is The Challenge’s Casey Cooper Today?

You might have missed a lot since the last time you saw Casey Cooper on the Fresh Meat. Today, this star is more than just a strong-willed contender. She is a wife and a soon-to-mother.

Yes, Casey tied the knot on December 7, 2021, with her long-time boyfriend Kyle Toups in Woodlands, Texas. “I’m not crying, you’re crying. #6yearsinthemaking,” she posted over IG on her wedding day. The series of pictures over the post featured Casey and her husband walking down the aisle and also making a toast in front of a decorated wedding cake.

Syrus Yarbrough, Casey’s co-star from The Ruins in 2009, commented on the post, “Congrats girl!!!!!! U look amazing.”

But if you’ve been following Casey over IG then their wedding shouldn’t be a surprise to you. This Fresh Meat star had been keeping her fans in the loop ahead of her big day ever since she received her marriage license in November that year.  “Less than a month away!” she captioned an Instagram Story at the time.

Reflecting on wedding plans, Casey went on to thank the incredible team of vendors (The Peach Orchard Venue, Adorne Artistry, Pineyrose Floral, Darkroux, and Wedandprosper) who helped them pull the wedding off in such a short time frame. “You all made this so special I can’t thank you enough for how above and beyond my wildest dreams everything turned out,” she wrote over IG.

Who Is Casey Cooper Husband, Kyle Toups?

Casey Cooper has known her husband Kyle Toups ever since 2015. Since then, the two often graced each other’s socials, and only grew closer each passing day.

Even during the 2020 pandemic, the two were quarantined together alongside Kyle’s son. That year, the three were also seen celebrating Thanksgiving together with their family, vacationing in Nuevo Vallarta.

Also, the next year, the couple kept up the tradition and celebrated Thanksgiving together with their family in Mexico. “I love these humans and this was such a good vacation away from work, wedding planning, the show, holiday stress, and baby planning. 10/10 recommend,” Casey wrote over IG at the time.

They were also expecting their baby then — which was due in early 2022.

As of 2021, Kyle lived in his hometown of Woodlands, Texas.

Here’s his Facebook @kyle.toups.33.

Digging around a little, we also found out that Kyle has relatives named Joseph and James Toups, and Erin Toups.

Casey Cooper Age

Casey Cooper was born on April 7, 1989. That made her 32 years of age in 2021.

As per her birthday, Casey is of the Aries zodiac.

Casey Cooper Job

Casey Cooper wasn’t always a reality star. It was only after her appearance at The Challenge Fresh Meat in 2006, she claimed to the international spotlight. Before that, she took on a job as an accountant. 

Also, after she exited the show completing The Ruins, she went back to her regular job. In April 2013, on her first day back at work, her co-workers welcomed her with a cake.

Talking about her time at The Challenge, Casey first joined the show its Fresh Meat season. She went on to compete on the show’s following seasons like  The Duel, The Gauntlet III, The Ruins, and All Stars 2.

As of 2021, she had already won $10,000 from The Challenge.

Initially, her motivator to win The Challenge was “breast implants!”

Did you know: Casey was the first competitor on Fresh Meat to admit that she wasn’t comfortable with people from different races, economic classes, or sexual preferences.
